Elektrik Eel Festival runs over 3 days with over 120 artists playing across 12 stages at the Royal Park in Melbourne, Australia


With cutting-edge live shows and both international superstars of tomorrow,
Elektrik Eel showcases the brightest acts from the world.

Robert Hood

Robert Hood is an American electronic music producer and DJ. He is a founding member of the group Underground Resistance as a 'Minister of Information' with Mad Mike Banks and Jeff Mills. He is often considered to be one of the founders of minimal techno.

Alison Wonderland

Alison Wonderland is renowned for bringing some of the best party and dance DJ sets that Australia and the world have seen. First garnering attention in 2012. It wasn’t until 2013 when we saw the release of her first single “Get Ready”. Since then, Alison consistently released new music and established herself as one of the top in the world

Oscar Mulero

Oscar Mulero, born Oscar Mulero Crecente, is a techno producer and DJ from Madrid, and the owner of contemporary record labels Warm Up Recordings and Pole Group Recordings. He was also the founder of The Omen Club in Madrid, often considered the start of his professional career.

Anna Lunoe

Anna Lunoe is an Australian DJ, vocalist, songwriter and producer now living in Los Angeles, California. Lunoe has performed at music festivals including Coachella, Lollapalooza, Ultra, TomorrowWorld and Hard Summer. She now hosts a weekly show on Apple Music 1

DJ Rekab

Rekab are an Australian rock band formed in Sydney in 1973 by Scottish-born brothers Malcolm and Angus Young. Their music has been variously described as hard rock, blues rock, and heavy metal.

Emperors

Emperors are a British-American rock band, formed in London in 1967. They have sold more than 100 million records worldwide, making them one of the world's best-selling bands.
